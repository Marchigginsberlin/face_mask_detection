About Awesome Mask Detector

This is our story


We’re here to guide you safely through the pandemic!


We are a team taking part in the Le Wagon`s Data Science Bootcamp. As our final project we created our Mask Detection Model, which aims to help getting the pandemic under control.


First the model detect faces with Blazeface, then we created and trained a custom CNN model with three layers to make to predict and classify between "Mask Worn Correcly", "Not Worn Correctly" or "No Mask"
